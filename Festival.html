Guling Guling Festival

Location

The Guling Guling Festival unfolds in the charming town of Paoay in Ilocos Norte, Philippines.

Cultural Tradition

Guling Guling is a deeply rooted tradition marking the beginning of the Catholic Lenten season. It's celebrated with cultural pride and religious significance.

Culinary Delights

The festival is also a feast for the senses with a variety of local delicacies and dishes to savor.

Date

The festival typically takes place in February or March, aligning with the lunar calendar and the Catholic season of Lent.
